About This Website

Hi! My name is Thomas Lorenz A. Baldivia, and I’m happy to welcome you to my personal website. This is a special space where you can get to know me better, who I am, where I come from, and the things that are important to me.

Here, you’ll find stories about my life, my family, and my experiences in school. I believe that every person has a unique journey, and this is mine. I grew up in San Jose, Antique, a place where the mountains meet the sea and where I’ve made so many memories. My family means the world to me, and I want to share a little bit about them, how they have shaped me, supported me, and helped me become the person I am today.

You can also read about my school life, from my elementary years to my current journey as a Grade 10 student in the Special Program in the Arts at Antique National School. School has been a big part of my life, filled with challenges, new friendships, and experiences that I will always carry with me.

This website is like a window into my world, and I hope that by exploring it, you’ll learn more about me, not just as a student but as a person. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy getting to know me.
