About Me

My name is Thomas Lorenz A. Baldivia. I was born on December 23, 2008, in San Jose, Antique, where the mountains meet the sea. My name has a special significance as it was inspired by my Lola Acay, who came from Amsterdam to visit the Philippines before I was born. I am the only son of Emerson Baldivia and Angie Baldivia. Growing up, my mother worked abroad as an OFW, while my father worked as a tricycle driver. During the times when my mother couldn't be there for me and my father was busy with work, my grandparents my Nanay (Lola) and Lolo became my second parents. They raised me, guided me, and supported my passion for the arts. My Nanay (Lola) and Lolo stepped in as my mother and father when my parents couldn't be there, and for that, I will always be grateful. I graduated from Asembleman Segundo Moscoso Memorial School in San Pedro, and I’m currently a Grade 10 SPA (Special Program in the Arts) student at Antique National School. Here, I continue to develop my skills and passion for the arts, particularly in visual arts. Being part of the SPA program has given me the opportunity to explore my creativity, participate in various art activities, and learn from experienced mentors. Aside from my studies, I enjoy spending time with my friends, playing basketball, and indulging in my favorite online games like Mobile Legends. These activities help me unwind and balance school with fun. Though I am not a fan of math, I try my best to get through it while focusing on the subjects that truly interest me. As I approach the end of my junior high school journey, I look forward to the new experiences and challenges ahead. My time at Antique National School has shaped me not only as a student but also as a person, and I trust that God has a wonderful plan for my life, and I am excited to see where He will lead me in the future.
